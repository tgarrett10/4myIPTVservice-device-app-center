Which Device Should You Choose?

Choose Onn Full HD when…

Your television is 1080p, cost is the main priority, or you are adding service to a secondary room.

Choose Onn 4K when…

You want the recommended option for most customers: dependable 4K performance, Google TV, strong value, and a device that is currently well stocked.

Choose Onn 4K Plus when…

You specifically want more storage, Wi-Fi 6, Dolby Vision, Dolby Atmos, and enhanced performance beyond the standard Onn 4K.

Choose Google TV Streamer when…

You prefer a premium Google device, need more storage, or want built-in Ethernet for a stable wired connection.

Choose NVIDIA Shield when…

You are a power user, have a home-theater setup, want AI upscaling, need USB expansion, or also use Plex and cloud gaming.

Already own a compatible device?

You may only need IPTV service and setup guidance. Use the service-only option instead of buying another device.

Still unsure?

Submit a request and select “Not Sure — Help Me Choose.” Your TV, Wi-Fi, usage, and budget will determine the recommendation.

Having an Issue? Start Here
If one of your apps is not working, use this page to reinstall the latest version for the app already included on your device.
What to do:
Find the app below and follow the note under it. Some apps only need to be reinstalled. Some apps need the old version deleted first, then reinstalled.
Important:
This page only includes the original side-loaded apps provided on your device. Any apps not shown here can be downloaded directly from the Google Play Store on your device. We only provide support for the apps listed in this App Library. We do not provide support for Play Store apps or apps installed from any other source.
Step 1
Find the app below Look for the app that is giving you trouble.
Step 2
Follow the note under that app Some apps can be reinstalled right away. Some need the old version deleted first.
Step 3
Install the latest version After reinstalling, open the app again and test it.

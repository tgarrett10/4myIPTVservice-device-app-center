Fix It Fast

Check Your Internet Confirm your device is online and your Wi-Fi is connected before trying anything else.
Check Your Login Re-enter your username, password, and server URL exactly as provided.
Restart the Device A quick restart often clears loading, buffering, and app response issues.
Simple first step: If you are not sure where the problem started, restart the device and then try logging in again carefully.

Best Support Flow

Step 1: Check Setup Go back through the Device Setup Center and confirm your device is online, the app is installed, and your credentials were entered exactly as provided.
Step 2: Use Support Bot First Use the Support Bot for first contact, to leave a message, or to explain the issue clearly before live follow-up.
Step 3: Move to Live Support If the problem continues or needs a direct conversation, use Live Telegram Support for person-to-person help.
Step 4: Include Useful Details For faster help, include your device type, the app you are using, and a screenshot of the issue when possible.

Most Common Issues

Buffering Restart the device, check Wi-Fi strength, and make sure the account is not being shared across too many screens.
Login Error Double-check every character in your username, password, and server URL. One mistake can prevent login.
No Channels or Content Refresh the playlist inside the app and allow the app a moment to load content after login.
App Will Not Open Restart the device, reopen the app, and confirm the installation completed properly.

Service Reminders

  • An active internet connection is required to use the service.
  • Your login credentials should be entered exactly as provided.
  • Sharing login credentials may cause buffering or service interruption.
  • Service works best on supported Android streaming devices.
  • Service can travel with you and be used anywhere you have stable internet, including hotels and vacation travel.

Quick Troubleshooting

  • Restart the streaming device.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Log out and log back into the app.
  • Refresh channels or playlist inside the app.
  • Try re-entering your username, password, and server URL carefully.
  • If problems continue, use one of the support options listed above.
If your device is offline or your login details are entered incorrectly, the app may not load properly.

How to Get Help

Use the support options above depending on what you need. The process is designed to get you help quickly and efficiently.

Start with the Support Bot for first contact. Move to Live Support if the issue requires direct help.

Best Support Flow

Step 2: Use Support Bot
Step 3: Move to Live Support if needed
Step 4: Provide device details and screenshots

Quick Troubleshooting

  • Restart your device
  • Check internet connection
  • Re-enter login details
  • Refresh app content
Incorrect login details or no internet will prevent the service from working.

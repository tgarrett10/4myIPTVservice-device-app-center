Service Reminders

  • An active internet connection is required to use the service.
  • Your login credentials should be entered exactly as provided.
  • Sharing login credentials may cause buffering or service interruption.
  • Service works best on supported Android streaming devices.
  • Service can travel with you and be used anywhere you have stable internet, including hotels and vacation travel.

Quick Troubleshooting

  • Restart the streaming device.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Log out and log back into the app.
  • Refresh channels or playlist inside the app.
  • If problems continue, use the support contact information listed above.
If your device is offline or your login details are entered incorrectly, the app may not load properly.

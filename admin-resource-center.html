Core Customer Workflow

Step 1: Customer Request

Customer submits request for service.

What to do: Monitor for new requests and respond quickly.

Go to Request Page

Step 2: Send Payment Link

Create and send payment link manually.

Critical: Do not allow changes after sending payment.

Go to Admin Dashboard

Step 3: Payment Received

Once payment is confirmed, move order forward.

What to do: Update status and prepare fulfillment.

Step 4: Fulfillment

Deliver service or prepare device.

Paths:

  • Online → Send login details
  • Device → Load apps + credentials

Step 5: Customer Setup

Guide customer to correct support page.

Device Support Online Support

Step 6: Track Customer

All customers must be logged.

Critical fields: Username, Password, Expiration Date

Open Customer Records

Step 7: Renewal Process

Monitor expiring customers and follow up.

Target: Reach out BEFORE expiration.

Open Calls Dashboard

Fix My Device

Try these quick steps first. Most issues are fixed in under 2 minutes.

🔧 Quick Fix (Start Here)

1. Unplug your device → wait 10 seconds → plug back in
2. Make sure your WiFi is connected
3. Close the app and reopen it

Still not working? Try one of the options below.

📺 App Not Working

1. Go to the home screen
2. Open the app again
3. If still not working → reinstall using Downloader
Open Downloader Codes

🌐 Buffering / Slow

1. Restart your internet modem/router
2. Move closer to WiFi
3. Close other apps running

📡 WiFi Not Connected

1. Go to Settings
2. Select Network
3. Reconnect to your WiFi

🎮 Remote Not Working

1. Remove batteries
2. Put them back in
3. Restart device

If it’s not fixed after trying these steps, reach out and I’ll help you quickly.

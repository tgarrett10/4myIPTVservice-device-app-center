Sideloaded Apps

What This Means

Sideloaded apps are apps installed from sources outside of the Google Play Store or Amazon Appstore. Some devices may require security or installer permissions to be adjusted before these apps can be installed.
Sideloaded apps are provided for convenience and can change more frequently than Play Store apps. At the time of setup or delivery, included sideloaded apps can be verified as working, but future availability may depend on the third-party app provider.
Because sideloaded apps come from outside official app stores, installation settings may need to be adjusted and ongoing app updates may require periodic review.

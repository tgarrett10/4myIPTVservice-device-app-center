Sideloaded Apps

What This Means

Sideloaded apps are apps installed from sources outside of the Google Play Store or Amazon Appstore. Some devices may require installer permissions to be adjusted before these apps can be installed.
Included sideloaded apps can be verified as working at the time of setup or delivery. Over time, app behavior and availability may change based on the third-party developer.
If a sideloaded app stops working later, updated versions or reinstall options may be needed from the customer support and self-help tools provided.

IPTV Setup Help

Quick Setup Guide for Your Streaming Device

1. Prepare Your Device

  1. Connect your device to the internet.
  2. From the home screen press the gear icon.
  3. Select All Settings.
  4. Go to Privacy.
  5. Scroll down to Security.
  6. Turn OFF Scan Apps with Play Protect.
  7. Turn OFF Improve Harmful App Detection.
Your streaming device must be connected to the internet to use IPTV services.

2. Install IPTV Smarters Pro

  1. Open the Google Play Store.
  2. Search for IPTV Smarters Pro.
  3. Install the application.
  4. Open the app once installation is complete.

3. Enter Your Login Information

  1. Select Login with Xtream Codes API.
  2. Enter the credentials provided by your installer:
    • Name – You can enter any name in this field. Many users simply enter IPTV.
    • Username – Provided to you by your installer.
    • Password – Provided to you by your installer.
    • Server URL – Provided to you by your installer.
  3. Press Login.
The login credentials must be entered exactly as they are provided to you.
Sharing your login credentials may cause your stream to buffer or stop working because multiple connections will attempt to use the same account.

4. First Launch Tips

  1. Allow the app a moment to load channels.
  2. Open the TV section to view live channels.
  3. You can add channels to Favorites.
  4. The Movies and Series sections contain on-demand content.

5. Troubleshooting

  1. Restart your streaming device.
  2. Check your internet connection.
  3. Log out and log back into the IPTV app.
  4. If channels do not load, refresh the playlist inside the app.
If problems continue, please contact support using the options on the main dashboard.

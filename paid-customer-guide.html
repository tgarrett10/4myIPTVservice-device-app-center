1. IPTV Smarters App

The main paid service app is the IPTV Smarters App. This is where your primary IPTV service is accessed. When you open the app, update all three sections:

  1. Live TV
  2. Movies
  3. Series

Place the cursor on each section, pull down to Update, and update that section.

Channel count: Depending on your setup, you may have English channels only, English and Spanish, or all available channels. Your channel count may range from approximately 7,500 to 20,000 channels.

Live TV Tips

  • The left column shows categories.
  • The middle column shows the channels inside the selected category.
  • The right side shows the live preview.

To watch full screen, select a channel and click the live preview box on the right.

Best practice: Go through the categories, find the channels you watch most, press and hold the center button on the remote, and add them to Favorites. This saves time and prevents searching through thousands of channels.

Other Helpful Options

  • Live with Guide: Browse live channels with the electronic program guide when available.
  • Multi-Screen: Watch multiple channels at one time.

2. Movies & Series

For Movies or Series, select All Movies or All Series first, then go up to the search option and type what you want to watch.

  • Press and hold the center button to add movies or shows to Favorites.
  • Anything you start watching may appear in Continue Watching.

4. Device Maintenance

Android devices can slow down if too many apps are left running in the background. If your device has Background Apps & Process List, use it about once per month.

  1. Open Background Apps & Process List.
  2. Select Close Multiple.
  3. Select each open app.
  4. Choose Force Stop.
  5. Press OK.
  6. Press the back button and repeat until open background apps are closed.

This helps free up processing power and keeps the device running smoother.

5. Downloader & Support Portal

Your device may include the Downloader app. This gives you access to my support portal.

  1. Open Downloader.
  2. Move the cursor all the way to the left.
  3. Move up to open the side menu.

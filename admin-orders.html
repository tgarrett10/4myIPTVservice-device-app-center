Internal Workflow

Next Steps After an Order Is Received

Do these steps in order

  1. Confirm the customer name, phone, email, requested device, and notes are readable.
  2. Review the requested setup and identify whether any clarification is needed before payment is sent.
  3. Set fulfillment type and update order status to reflect where the order stands.
  4. Generate and send the correct payment or checkout link.
  5. Update payment status once payment is sent and again once payment is received.
  6. Track delivery or setup progress so nothing gets lost after payment.
  7. Move completed jobs to COMPLETE only after the order is fully closed out.

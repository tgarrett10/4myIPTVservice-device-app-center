Quick Start

What You Need Before You Start

1. Your Device Make sure your ONN device, Firestick, or Android-based streaming device is connected to your TV and powered on.
2. Internet Connection Your device must be connected to Wi-Fi or another stable internet source before the app will work.
3. Login Details Keep your username, password, and server URL nearby so you can enter them exactly as provided.
Step 1

Prepare Your Device

  • Connect the device to your TV and power.
  • Connect to Wi-Fi during first setup.
  • Open Settings and complete your ONN device preparation steps.
  • Turn off any Play Protect or harmful app scanning settings if your device requires it.
Step 2

Install Downloader

  • Open the Play Store on your Android streaming device.
  • Search for Downloader.
  • Install the app.
  • Downloader is useful for alternate installs and future app recovery if needed.
Step 3

Log Into IPTV Smarters Pro

  • Select Login with Xtream Codes API.
  • Name can be anything, such as IPTV.
  • Username, Password, and Server URL must match exactly as provided.
  • Do not share your credentials. Shared logins can cause buffering or service interruption.
After setup: If an app ever stops working later, use the App Library to reinstall or update it.

Before you begin
You need a stable internet connection, a supported Android-based streaming device, and the login credentials provided by your installer. This setup is designed to be simple and easy to follow.

Recommended order
1. Prepare your device settings
2. Install IPTV Smarters Pro
3. Enter your credentials exactly as provided
4. Open Support / Tips if you need help
Step 1

Prepare Your Device

  • Connect the device to your TV and power.
  • Connect to Wi-Fi during first setup.
  • Open Settings and complete your ONN device preparation steps.
  • Turn off any Play Protect or harmful app scanning settings if your device requires it.
Step 2

Install Downloader App

  • Open the Play Store on your Android streaming device.
  • Search for Downloader.
  • Install the app.
  • Use Downloader when you need to open installer links and app codes.
Step 3

Log Into IPTV Smarters Pro

  • Select Login with Xtream Codes API.
  • Name can be anything, such as IPTV.
  • Username, Password, and Server URL must match exactly as provided.
  • Do not share your credentials. Shared logins can cause buffering or service interruption.
Step 4

Common Fixes

Buffering Restart the device, check Wi-Fi strength, and make sure the account is not being shared.
Login Errors Re-enter the username, password, and server URL exactly as provided.
No Channels Refresh the playlist inside the app and allow the app a moment to load content.
Important: This service requires internet access. If your device is offline, the app and channels will not load correctly.
